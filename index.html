How to Use

Perfect tile leveling and clean alignment—follow the steps and tips below.

Step-by-step Guide

  1. 1
    Insert the clip

    Place the clip into the bottom of the tile.

  2. 2
    Align under the next tile

    Slide clips under the tile and add the next tile to continue the row.

  3. 3
    Insert wedge with tool

    Use the provided tool to insert and set the wedge for leveling.

  4. 4
    Tap and finalize

    Use the rubber mallet gently to lock the level alignment.

  5. 5
    Remove after set

    After the mortar settles (around 2–4 hours), remove spacers carefully.

Placement Tips
Keep consistent spacing, press tiles evenly, and remove wedges carefully to protect tile edges.
